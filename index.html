Design for Accessibility

Squirl Sign Case Competition · February 27th

In this competition, you will be designing an app (mobile or tablet) for Squirl Signs, a UofT student-led startup developing a tool aiding deaf/hard-of-hearing (HoH) individuals who use sign language to communicate with hearing individuals.

Your goal is to design a high-fidelity prototype of Squirl Sign's app for deaf/HoH users. The prototype will focus on specific features outlined under Competition Requirements. You will present your designs and user research at our in-person competition on February 27th.
