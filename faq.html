Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm not confident with public speaking?

This is a great opportunity to build your public speaking skills.

What if I have no prior design or coding experience?

That's okay! We encourage participation regardless of skill level.

Do I need to be enrolled in a CCIT program?

No! The competition is open to all programs/majors.

Can I work alone?

Yes! You do not need a team.

Can I attend without submitting a product?

Yes! You are free to attend as a general member.
