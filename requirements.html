Competition Requirements

Note: Your mockups must have a minimum of 4 screens (but can have more).

Presentation Slides

Participants must create a slide-deck presentation that includes their final design. The presentation should highlight their approach to the app's Onboarding Process, Translation Screen, and at least one other feature of your choice from below.

User Research

Include in your presentation at least one method of user research you employed (and the insight it brought you) to justify your design decisions.

Core Features (mobile or tablet app)

Onboarding Process Mandatory

  • Option to sign-up
  • Lets users set up their profile and account (e.g. asks if they are deaf, partially hearing, or hearing)

Dashboard

  • Overview of all features in the app
  • Summary of the user profile
  • Highlights of user actions and incomplete activities

Translation Screen Mandatory

  • Camera feed + real-time text translation
  • Accessibility settings (option to adjust font size, brightness, speed, view previous translations)
  • Translates signs to English and translates speech into sign language with an avatar

Other Features (Choose at least 1)

Accessibility Settings

  • Adjustable text size, contrast, light/dark mode
  • Visual indicators for user actions

Profile

  • Overview of a deaf/HoH user's profile, including their answers from the onboarding process

You may propose your own feature

  • What could the app benefit from including?
  • What new feature does your research support?

Important Dates

Deadline February 26 at 11:59 PM EST

The final design submission is due.

Presentation Time Maximum 7 minutes

Contestants will have a maximum of 7 minutes to present their designs and explain the process behind their work. To keep on schedule, participants who go over this time will need to be cut off.
